Interested in joining?

If you are a graduate student in Geotechnical Engineering at The University of Texas and you're interested in joining our organization please send us an e-mail and we will send you membership application. Annual student membership to our organization costs $15. Of this $15, $8 will be used to create t-shirts and $7 will be used to pay for your food at meetings and/or Grand Rounds events.
